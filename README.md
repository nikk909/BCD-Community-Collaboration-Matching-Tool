## Overview

The BCD Community Collaboration Matching Tool is an intelligent partner-finding system that helps community members quickly locate collaboration partners based on keywords. The system shows how well potential partners match your needs and how frequently they've discussed related topics. After collaboration, members can rate each other to build "collaboration credit scores," making it easier to find reliable partners in the future.

### Core Concept

In the BCD community, use **"keywords"** to quickly find collaboration partners. You can see:
- How well they match your needs
- How many times they've discussed related topics

Choose who you want to work with, and the system will set up a temporary group. After collaborating, rate each other to earn "collaboration credit scores"‚Äîmaking it easier to find reliable partners later.

## How It Works

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Send a Request**: Share a keyword + how you want to collaborate via WhatsApp
   - Example: "Looking for someone to chat about 'solar investment' for a 1-on-1 discussion"
   - Example: "Looking for 3 people to do 'Crypto joint investment'"

2. **Automatic Matching**: The system automatically finds community members who:
   - Often talk about this topic
   - Match your needs
   - Shows you "who fits best and how many times they've discussed related content"

3. **Pick Partners**: Review the matching list and select the people you think are right

4. **Automatic Group Creation**: Once they agree, a temporary group is created automatically for direct communication

5. **Rate & Score**: After the collaboration, rate each other. Scores are stored in your community account‚Äîhigher scores mean you'll be more likely to be found by others in the future

## Usage Guide

### 3 Simple Steps to Use

1. **Send a Request**: Share a keyword + how you want to collaborate via WhatsApp
   - Format: "Looking for [number] people to [type of collaboration] about '[keyword]'"
   - Example: "Looking for 3 people to do 'Crypto joint investment'"

2. **Pick Partners**: Review the matching list and select the people you think are right

3. **Chat + Rate**: Communicate in the group and rate each other after the collaboration

### Supported Collaboration Types

- **1-on-1 Discussion**: Direct one-on-one conversations about specific topics
- **Group Discussion**: Multi-person discussions (specify number of participants)
- **Joint Investment**: Form investment groups for specific projects
- **Custom**: Other collaboration types as needed

## Technical Details

### Matching Algorithm

The system considers:
- **Keyword relevance**: How closely the member's discussions match the requested keyword
- **Discussion frequency**: How often the member has discussed related topics
- **Collaboration credit score**: Historical rating from previous collaborations
- **Active participation**: Recent activity in the community

### Rating System

- Members rate each other after collaboration completion
- Ratings contribute to "collaboration credit scores"
- Higher scores increase visibility in future matching
- Scores are stored in community accounts
